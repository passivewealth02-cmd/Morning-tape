# Maintena — Go-To-Market Kit

Positioning (from the PRD):
- ❌ Don't say: "AI property management software"
- ✅ Do say: "Stop losing maintenance requests and chasing vendors manually."
- One-liner: **The AI operations layer for property maintenance.**

## Ideal Customer Profile
- Small-to-mid property management companies, **50–2,000 units**
- Geography: **Canada + United States**
- Types: residential property managers, condo management firms, apartment operators, rental portfolio managers
- Buyers/titles: Operations Manager, Property Manager, Maintenance Coordinator, Owner/Principal

## The offer (kills the setup objection)
> "Free 14-day trial — and I'll set up your entire portfolio for you. Bulk-import your
> properties, generate a QR code for every unit, done in an afternoon."

## Channels (Phase 1 = manual outbound)
- LinkedIn (search / Sales Navigator) — target titles above in target cities
- NARPM member directory (US residential PMs)
- Google Maps → "property management {city}" → owner/ops contact
- Facebook property-management + landlord groups
- BiggerPockets forums
- Reddit: r/PropertyManagement, r/Landlord

## Cold email sequence

**Email 1**
Subject: losing maintenance requests at {Company}?

Hi {First} — quick question: how do maintenance requests come in at {Company} right now —
calls, texts, email, a spreadsheet?

Most managers running 50–500 units tell me requests slip through the cracks and half the day
goes to chasing vendors for status.

Maintena fixes exactly that: tenants scan a QR code on their door, AI triages the issue in
seconds and auto-dispatches the right vendor — you just approve. Tenants get updates
automatically and can track it themselves.

Worth a 15-min look? I'll set up your properties for free so you see it on your own units.

— {You}

**Email 2 (day +3)**
Bumping this — if "which vendor did we send to 4B, and did they show?" sounds familiar,
that's the chaos this kills. 10-min demo on your portfolio?

**Email 3 (day +5, breakup)**
Closing the loop — if maintenance coordination isn't a headache right now, ignore me. If
you're still chasing vendors by phone, I can show you a faster way in 10 minutes.
